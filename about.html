ABOUT

Rudi Rizaldi or commonly called Aldi, born in Kudus, Central Java on February 18, 1998, is a student majoring in electrical engineering at the University of Jember, East Java. Has worked as an employee at one of the leading BUMN companies in Indonesia, namely Peruri. Peruri is a company engaged in the printing sector. The products that they work on include printing money from the Republic of Indonesia and other important documents that have a high level of security.
